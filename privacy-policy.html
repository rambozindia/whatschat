Privacy Policy

Number Status Download

Effective Date: March 23, 2026 | Last Updated: March 23, 2026

BlueBurn Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Number Status Download mobile application (formerly known as "Number to WhatsChat Status"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone Numbers: When you use the direct messaging feature, phone numbers you enter are stored locally on your device for your recent history and favorites. These numbers are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Theme Preferences: Your dark mode preference is stored locally on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and ad personalization.
  • Log Data: In case of errors, we collect diagnostic data including IP address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, time and date of usage, and other statistics.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

1.3 Information Stored Locally

  • Status History Cache: The app caches status media files (photos and videos) locally on your device for offline viewing. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any server. You can clear this cache at any time from the app settings.
  • Recent Numbers & Favorites: Phone numbers you message and mark as favorites are stored locally using device storage. This data is not transmitted externally.

2. Permissions We Request

The app requests the following device permissions:

  • Storage / Media Access: Required to read and save status media files (photos and videos) from your device.
  • Camera: Used exclusively for the QR code scanner feature to scan QR codes containing phone numbers. No photos or videos are captured or stored through this permission.
  • Internet: Required for displaying advertisements and opening external links.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (direct messaging, status downloading)
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and fix errors
  • To store your preferences (theme, favorites) locally

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements

These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies governing how they use your information. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All user data (phone numbers, favorites, preferences, cached statuses) is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.
  • We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Through third-party ad services (Google AdMob) as described above
  • If required by law or to comply with legal processes

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. External Links

The app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: Clear your recent numbers, favorites, and cached statuses from within the app at any time.
  • Revoke permissions: You can revoke any permission (storage, camera) from your device settings at any time.
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all locally stored data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
